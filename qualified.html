General Guidelines:

Please find below the guidelines for participation:
1. Carry college ID Cards.Each team should bring atleast one laptop compulsorily.
2. Breakfast and lunch will be provided for all team members.
3. WiFi and electrical power points will be provided.
4. All the team members to report on 29th April 2024 at 8:00 am for registration followed by  breakfast.
‍
The rules of the competition:
1.You must treat all team members, competitors, judges, coaches, volunteers, etc., with respect and courtesy, abiding by the code of conduct.
2. All team members should be present at the event.
3. All work on a project should be done during the hackathon at the venue “ONLY”.
4. Teams must stop hacking once the time is up.  
5. Teams can be disqualified from the competition at the organizer’s discretion. Reasons might include but are not limited to breaking the Competition Rules, behaving in a way that violates the code of conduct, or other unsporting behavior.
6. Students should be well-dressed and well-groomed.
